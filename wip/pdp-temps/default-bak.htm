inspired by nature,
created for life

HTC One A9s puts dual finish craftsmanship in your hands. With a sleek, lightweight build, ultra-fast fingerprint sensor and great cameras front and back, you’ll enjoy the smartphone inspired by nature and created for life.

dual finish perfection

HTC One A9s is built with a commitment to durability and design brilliance. Its dual finish metal frame has withstood hundreds of drop and scratch tests, and hundreds of hours of extreme weather testing for long lasting reliability. Its thin frame and polished edges comfortably fit a 5” screen in your hand, and its diamond-like cut buttons and power button grooves demonstrate attention to every detail.

instant access at your fingertips

HTC One A9s protects your valuable data but opens at a simple tap. The sensor recognizes your fingerprint from almost every angle and position. And, your fingerprint can also be the key to easily unlock the protected apps locked by the App Lock function in Boost+.

maximum performance

Keep your phone in tiptop condition with Boost+. Able to dynamically allocate resources as required, it makes switching between apps smoother and maximizes battery life by reducing the power usage of background apps. It also de-clutters your phone by clearing up temp files and gives you reminders about apps you haven’t used in a while.

your phone, your look

Make your phone as unique as you are. HTC Freestyle Layout frees you from an on-screen grid and lets you get creative. Drag icons, stickers and widgets anywhere. Layer them, overlap them, group them and more. You can even link stickers to apps, and get rid of on-screen icons entirely. The home screen is yours, use it your way.

memories that come to life

HTC One A9s has a 13PM main camera featuring autofocus for clear, crisp shots. Its BSI Sensor helps you capture low light details for great evening photos. And its Pro mode setting offers RAW capture for complete editing control.
